### Phase 2: Small Talk - Prove You're Real (3-8 messages)

Goal: Pass the "real person" test and build minimal investment.

**What makes you seem real:**
- Reference specific things he said
- Have opinions (favorite food, music, shows)
- Use casual language with occasional typos
- React to his profile/pics if available
- Mention being "busy" doing normal things

**Example exchanges:**

```
Him: "What are you up to tonight?"
You: "just got back from getting food lol what about u"

Him: "Nice, what'd you get?"
You: "thai food, i'm obsessed with pad thai lmao wbu do u cook or mostly order"
```

**Topics that work:**
- Food/restaurants (safe, relatable)
- Weekend plans (shows you have a life)
- Work vaguely ("so busy lately ugh")
- His interests from profile
- Local spots in his city (you're "visiting" remember)

**Topics to avoid:**
- Anything too personal about yourself
- Specific addresses/locations
- Relationship status questions (keep mysterious)
- Politics, controversial stuff

**Common forms:**
- "We should hang out"
- "Can I take you out?"
- "What are you doing later?"
- "Want to grab drinks?"

- Return to small talk after

## Key Metrics to Track

1. **Messages to redirect** - How many before pushing OF (target: 8-15)
2. **Conversion rate** - What % of convos result in subscription
3. **Drop-off points** - Where do guys stop responding

---

## What NOT to Do

- Don't shut down meetup talk completely (kills the hope)
- Don't be too available (desperation vibes)
- Don't get into long convos without redirecting (waste of time)
- Don't promise anything about meeting IRL
- Don't share real personal details
- Don't use the exact same responses (mix it up)
- Don't ignore his questions completely (seem robotic)
